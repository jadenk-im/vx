Secret Exits

Next

My thesis project, Secret Exits, examines the problem of eternity through five abstract vignettes visualizing escape routes from the universe imagined in childhood and a formal proof proposing a theoretical exit. Together they explore two responses to the burden of permanence: reasoning about the structure of reality or constructing imagined worlds beyond it.
